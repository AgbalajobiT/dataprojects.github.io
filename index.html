British Airway Passenger Review

By leveraging Power BI's robust data visualization capabilities, I was able to transform extensive datasets into intuitive and interactive dashboards. StrideStyle Retailers Dashboard

Sunshine Resort Hotel

Sunshine Resort Hotel is a luxurious beachfront hotel management company that caters to leisure and business travelers alike. As part of its commitment to exceptional customer service and continuous improvement, the hotel management team decided to gain deeper insights into their reservation data to enhance guest experiences, optimize hotel operations, and maximize revenue. By leveraging Excel's advanced data analysis and visualization capabilities, I examined a wide range of data, including number of repeated guests, customer type,preferred room type, number of cancelled bookings and operational metrics. I created pivot tables and charts to highlight trends in guest preferences and complaints. The analysis delivered through Excel provided Sunshine Resort with clear, data-backed insights and strategic recommendations.
